About Me

Developer. Musician. Community Builder. Problem Solver.

Hi, I'm Nicolas Graham — a recent graduate from the University of South Florida with a BS in Applied Science, concentration in Information Architecture. I'm passionate about building technology that makes a difference in people's lives.

My Panamanian heritage taught me the power of community — that we rise together, not alone. Now I build technology that brings people together and creates safe spaces for those who need them. 🇵🇦💙

From mental health applications to IT management systems, I create solutions that are both functional and meaningful. When I'm not coding, you'll find me composing ambient music that soothes the soul or capturing peaceful moments through photography.

I also moderate a Discord community of 500+ members, where I've learned the importance of creating safe, supportive digital spaces. This experience shapes how I approach every project — with empathy, intention, and care for the people who will use what I build.

Currently based in Tampa, FL 🌴, I'm seeking entry-level opportunities in IT and web development where I can contribute my skills, grow as a developer, and continue building technology that genuinely helps people.
